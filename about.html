Hi, I'm Long

I’m a front-end developer and self-taught designer.

In the year of 2017, I graduated from a software engineer bachelor degree. During my studying time, I had a strong interest with Human-computer interaction design as it’s a part of my study.

After graduation, I had been working as a developer in several companies in Vietnam. Till late 2018, I found myself going back to my real passion in interaction design. In particularly, I have been educating myself and working as a UX/UI Designer ever since.

Being a designer who is able to understand the software system like an engineer, I believe i can bring up lots of interesting ideas to create more efficient digital products.

Design Process

What I Do

Strategy

Ask first. Before jumping into design works I always make sure that we’re asking the right questions and setting right challenges to solve. This stage includes market research, competitive analyzing, consulting and exploring possible solutions.

Design

Problem solving. At the end of this phase, you will have a pixel perfect design for your app or website. During the transition from wireframes into the final design, I create prototypes simulating final end results before development.

Development

‘Bring the designs to life!’. Since I’m a designer who can also code, I find it easy to collaborate closely with developers til the finish line and beyond. For Website and App Mobile projects, I also do front-end development.

Quality assurance

Transparent process. Complete overview of my design process through regular meetings. After the initial design version, I’m happy to do user testing with your customers to ensure the best possible solutions for their needs.

My skills

By attending workshops, reading blogs and applying observed knowlege to my projects in my spare time, I have learnt a lot of processes, tools and theories of UX/UI. I hope to further my career in the product design field.
